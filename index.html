My Story

About Me

Hi, I'm Prashma Shah. By profession, I'm a doctor, but beyond the hospital walls I live in a world of stories, emotions, and characters.

Reading has always been my escape — a place where imagination, emotions, and perspectives come alive. What started as a personal passion eventually turned into @read.with.prashma, a growing community of readers who love discovering meaningful stories together.

For me, books are not just stories — they are experiences that stay with you forever.
